Welcome! Who is Mr. Soper?

I graduated from Valdosta State University in 2014 with a degree in Criminal Justice. After working at the Boys and Girls Club for a few years, I decided to pursue a career in teaching. I currently teach 8th-grade Georgia Studies at Eighth Street Middle School in Tift County, Georgia.

Growing up and throughout college, becoming a teacher was never part of the plan. If you told 20-year-old Mr. Soper that he would be a teacher in 5 years, he would have never believed you. However, after gaining experience working with young people at the Boys and Girls Club, I found that I enjoyed building positive relationships and helping people reach their goals. Being a teacher combines those joys with my love of history. I think I finally found what I want to be when I grow up.

About this site

This site is designed for any students, parents, or teachers to utilize any resources to help learn Georgia Studies. You will find a syllabus, schedule, an extra lesson, and helpful links. If you have any suggestions for improvements, please email: zachary.soper@tiftschools.com
